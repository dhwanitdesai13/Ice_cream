About Us

Treat Ice Cream was won by taste, innovation and quality. It’s a difference you can taste. Which is why we are so loved by our fans and continue to make new ones? Keeping with the times and changing tastes of customers, Treat Ice Cream, as an ice cream brand has come a long way. Fast moving ahead with its path-breaking innovations, strategic roadmap and fruitful partnerships, Treat is all set to become Central India's most loved ice cream brand.

All the standards of hygiene and quality control are observed at these plants. Treat has always adopted the latest and the most suitable technique for preparing its ice creams. The motto behind this is to give its customers the best products and retain a satisfactory smile on their faces. Treat scoops out lip-smacking variety of 100% vegetarian ice creams, sundaes, candy bars and novelties.

Ice cream is a mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and sometimes other ingredients that has been frozen into a soft, creamy delight using special techniques. Ice cream has been a popular treat for hundreds of years but has only become commonplace since the widespread use of refrigeration. The exploding popularity of ice cream has led to a number of ice-cream variations including frozen custard, frozen yogurt, and even non-dairy versions made with ingredients like coconut milk.
